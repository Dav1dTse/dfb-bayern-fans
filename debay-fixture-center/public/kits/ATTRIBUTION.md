## Match Kit Assets

The transparent group-stage match kit cutouts in `matches/` were derived from FIFA World Cup 2026 match coordination graphics shared by Footy Headlines:

https://www.footyheadlines.com/2026/06/all-71-2026-world-cup-group-stage-kit.html

Generated locally for this fixture guide so each group-stage match uses the specific Team A / Team B player uniform combination shown in the coordination graphics.
